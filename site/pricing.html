// Common Questions

What if my project falls between two tiers?

Most do. The tiers are starting points. I'll scope your project during our first conversation and land on a fixed price that matches the actual work.

What does "your team is involved" actually mean?

One or two people from your team work alongside me for the duration. They're actively building, and by the end they understand the system well enough to modify, fix, and extend it on their own.

Do I need IT infrastructure?

For the Training tier, I deploy on hardware you already have, often a spare desktop or mini PC. The Turnkey tier includes purpose-built server hardware delivered and configured for your environment. It's all designed to be straightforward.

What happens after the engagement ends?

You keep everything: the code, the documentation, the skills. Post-launch support is included in every tier for follow-up questions and minor adjustments. After that, your team is equipped to keep going independently. If you want ongoing support, I can arrange that separately.

Can I sell the software I build?

Yes. You own the IP completely. If you build a tool that solves a problem other shops have, you're free to license it, sell it, or white-label it. Several clients have turned their project into a recurring revenue stream.
