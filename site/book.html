// What To Include In Your Email

No formal template needed — but the more I know going in, the better I can prepare for our call. A few things that help:

  • Your name and role e.g. Operations Manager, Plant Engineer, Owner.
  • Your company And the best phone number to reach you on, if you'd prefer a call back.
  • Your industry CNC machining, metal fabrication, plastics / injection molding, food processing, packaging, wood products, contract manufacturing — or something else.
  • Number of employees Under 20, 20–50, 50–100, 100–200, or 200+.
  • What you're dealing with Describe the process, the pain, the spreadsheet, the workaround. Whatever you've been thinking about replacing.
  • What the tool would look like if it existed You've probably been sketching this in your head for a while. What does it do? Who uses it? What changes when it's running?
